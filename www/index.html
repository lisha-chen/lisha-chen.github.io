About me

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tianyi Chen. I obtained my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from RPI in 2021, and my B.S. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017. My broad research interests include machine learning, optimization, and statistical signal processing. My current research focuses on the theory and applications of multi-objective learning and meta-learning.

I will join the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Rochester in 2025 as a tenure-track Assistant Professor. I am looking for self-motivated students. Fully funded Ph.D. positions are available starting from Fall 2025. If you are interested, please check out more details here.
