About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tianyi Chen. I obtained my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from RPI in 2021, and my B.S. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017. My broad research interests include machine learning, optimization, and statistical signal processing. My current research focuses on the theory and applications of multi-objective learning and meta-learning.
