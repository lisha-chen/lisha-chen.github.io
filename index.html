About me

I am now pursuing my PhD at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tianyi Chen.

My broad research interests include machine learning, optimization, and statistical signal processing. My current research focuses on the theory and applications of multi-objective learning and meta-learning.
